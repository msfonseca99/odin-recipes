Francesinha

Description

Learn how to do a francesinha in 30 min.

Ingredients

  • garlic
  • Porto Wine
  • corn starch
  • salt
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • white wine
  • beer
  • tomato
  • red chilli
  • bread
  • olive oil
  • small onion

Steps

  1. Toast the slices of bread. Cut the sausage in half lengthways.
  2. Season the steak with salt and pepper, put the frying pan on the heat with the butter and fry the steak on both sides. Set aside.
  3. Assemble the francesinha in the following order: slice of bread, ham, sausage, steak, slice of bread and the cheese covering the francesinha. Set aside.
  4. Saute the chopped onion in olive oil, add the chopped garlic, chilli and bay leaf.
  5. Add the tomatoes, beer, wine, water, Worcestershire sauce and beef stock. Leave to simmer for 10 minutes. Season with salt.
  6. Add the cornflour diluted in cold water and stir in. Finally add the port wine and boil for 5 minutes. Remove from the heat and blend with a hand blender.
  7. Pour 3 tablespoons of sauce over the francesinha and bake in the oven at 180ÂºC on the grill for 5 minutes.
  8. Pour the remaining sauce over the francesinha.
  9. Crack the egg into a frying pan with the olive oil and place on top of the francesinha.
