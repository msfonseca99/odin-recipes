Mini pizza made in Air fryer

Description

Try this Air Fryer mini pizza: nutritious, tasty and perfect for a balanced and healthy diet.

Ingredients

  • tomato
  • courgette
  • pepper
  • cheese
  • mushrooms

Steps

  1. Place the yoghurt and flour in a bowl and mix to form a dough.
  2. Roll out the dough on a baking tray and, with the help of a mould, make a circle.
  3. Spread on the tomato pulp. Decorate with mushrooms, peppers and courgettes.
  4. Season with salt and 5 grams of pepper. Add the grated mozzarella cheese.
  5. Place in the Air Fryer.
